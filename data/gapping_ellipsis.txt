El periódico Juan lo compró, y la revista Luis ___.
----
El periódico Juan lo compró, y la revista Luis la compró.
# Source: https://www.glossa-journal.org/article/id/7993/
# Translation eng: Juan bought the newspaper, and Luis (bought) the magazine.

#Translation: At the bar, Pablo ordered a beer and Juan (ordered) a whiskey

Mi madre tomó un té, y creo que mi padre ___ un café
----
Mi madre tomó un té, y creo que mi padre tomó un café
#Source: Jung, W., (2022) “Phasehood and embedded gapping in Spanish”, Glossa: a journal of general linguistics 7(1). doi: https://doi.org/10.16995/glossa.7993
#Translation: My mother drank a tea and I think that my father (drank) a coffee.

#Translation: Juan gave Susana some flowers, and he believed that Luis (gave) María some books.
