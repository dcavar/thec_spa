# Notes:
# The constructions with gapping are all ungrammatical. The full forms are all grammatical.
# The assumed elided positions are indicated by ___.

Juan fue al cine hoy y Ana ___ teatro mañana. 
----
Juan fue al cine hoy y Ana va a ir teatro mañana.
#Source: Jung, Wonsuk. (2022). Phasehood and embedded gapping in Spanish. Glossa: a journal of general linguistics. 7. 10.16995/glossa.7993. 
#Translation: Juan went to the cinema today and Ana will go to the theather tomorrow. 
# Note: Gapping does not tolerate tense mismatch between the ellipsis site and the antecedent

Escribí la carta a Luisa y la ___ a Cristina.
----
Escribí la carta a Luisa y la carta a Cristina.
# Source: Kester, Ellen-Petra, and Petra Sleeman. "N-ellipsis in Spanish." Linguistics in the Netherlands 19.1 (2002): 107-116.
# Note: NP ellipsis is ungrammatical when the modifier isn't introduced by 'de'

Compré el regalo para Isabel y el ___ para Jaime.
----
Compré el regalo para Isabel y el regalo para Jaime.
# Source: Kester, Ellen-Petra, and Petra Sleeman. "N-ellipsis in Spanish." Linguistics in the Netherlands 19.1 (2002): 107-116.
# Note: NP ellipsis is ungrammatical when the modifier isn't introduced by 'de'
